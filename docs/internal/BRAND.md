## Logo

Lifebuoy symbol representing support.

- **Light background**: Lifebuoy in Tangerine (#FFA400), wordmark in Espresso (#201B13)
- **Dark background**: Lifebuoy in Tangerine (#FFA400), wordmark in White (#FFFFFF)
- **Clearspace**: Buffer one lifebuoy width around full logo, one segment around icon alone

Assets: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1QAvlGGan64dFr54on7EaFkAsNpw6iYbB

## Typography

- **Primary**: Calibre
- **Fallback**: Inter Display Medium (headlines), Inter Regular/Italic (body)

## Graphic Elements

### Inverted Buoy Pattern

- Use SVG/EPS for scalable output, PNG up to 1000x1425px max
- Rotate in 90° increments only (0°, 90°, 180°, 270°)
- Can flip horizontally or vertically
- Opacity: 30-50%
- Colors: approved brand colors only
- **Don't**: Rotate at non-90° angles, stretch, or skew

## Copywriting (UI Text)

- Clear & concise, customer benefit first
- Human tone, not corporate
- **Use**: support, scale, on-demand, experts, brands, partners
- **Avoid**: cheap (use affordable), speedy (use efficient), buzzwords, unexplained acronyms
